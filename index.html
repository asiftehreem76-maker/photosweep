Privacy Policy

Your photos stay yours.

Data We Collect

None. PhotoSweep does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no ads.

Photo Library Access

PhotoSweep requests access to your Photo Library to display your photos and videos for review. This access is used solely to:

  • Display photos and videos for you to swipe through
  • Move items you choose to bin into the iOS deletion queue
  • Show file sizes so you can see how much storage you'll free

Your photos never leave your device. PhotoSweep does not upload, copy, or transmit any images or videos anywhere.

Local Storage

PhotoSweep stores a small amount of data on your device using UserDefaults:

  • IDs of photos you've already reviewed (so you don't see them again)
  • Lifetime stats (total freed storage, deleted count)

This data stays on your device and is never transmitted. You can reset it anytime from within the app.

Third-Party Services

PhotoSweep uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, crash reporters, or ad networks. There is zero network activity.

Children's Privacy

PhotoSweep does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.
